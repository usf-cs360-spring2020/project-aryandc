Why I choose this dataset?

I chose SF Police Department Incident Reports dataset because, I wanted to figure out weather the crime is reduced due to the current circumstance in San Francisco, and what incident type is prevalent in each neighborhood.
Also, this dataset has over 337k incident records for -

  • 75 different types of crimes
  • 15 neighborhoods
  • 11 district
